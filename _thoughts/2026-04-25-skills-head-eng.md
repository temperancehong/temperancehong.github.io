This is a doc about what I learned and observed from my new head of engineering.

## Daily maintainance

- Write what is done yesterday, to-do for today (with claer objectives) BEFORE the daily stand up.
- Please update your Linear tickets!
- Is it written down? All your thoughts, ideas, what you have tried should be documented. 
- Little game at the end of daily stand up when schedule is not too tight: Geoguessr etc.

## Product Sense

- How will this client project help build our own product/platform? We eventually need to only take projects that help nourrish our own product.
- The clients they care about FINAL results, not how you arrived there. And WOW effects.
- Take a step back: do we really need to do it in this workflow? We can change the workflow as well.
- Take on multiple projects. Client projects and our own product platform. They compliment each other.

## 1-1 Talks
- It is okay to take RTT
- Ask for help when you are blocked, it is helping the team
- Share your skills/hacks frequently
